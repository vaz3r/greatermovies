Best Netflix Movies to Watch: Late August 2018

GREATER MOVIES 08/27/2018

Have you been wondering what's the best movies on Netflix right now?
Don't wonder at all! As we have collected, curated, and ranked a list of the 50 best Netflix movies to watch late August 2018.

We have ranked the best Netflix movies using both IMDB and RottenTomatoes Community Critics. To be included in our list of the best of Netflix movies, titles must have a rating (60% or higher) on RottenTomatoes and (6/10 or higher) on IMDB, movies must also have over 20 eligible reviews to be considered.
